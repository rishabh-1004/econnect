About US

An idea that skimmed over a lunch meet-up, E-Connect is the next big step in heralding the ever-growing Startup culture. An initiative to facilitate all attributes and aspects related to the successful expansion of a startup or an idea, we aim to bring it all under one roof and smoothify the path of entrepreneurial development.

Our Idea and Undertaking

At the very basic level of our idea is our fondness for the entrepreneurial atmosphere and our will to help its expansion. Being a group of like-minded people, we have come across startup stories and came to know of the privations they had to overcome on way to their success. This led us to the inkling of making a one-stop solution for the startup culture. E-Connect is a networking platform bringing together Startups, Freelancers, Mentors, Investors, Experienced and Motivated Alumni, and also Interns, putting all under one roof to help them E-Connect. There is something for everyone on our platform.
